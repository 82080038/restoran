# RESEARCH_30: Emerging Technologies (Robotics, AR/VR, Blockchain)

## Research Overview

This research explores emerging technologies transforming the restaurant industry, including robotics and automation, augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) dining experiences, and blockchain technology for supply chain and payments. The research covers current applications, future trends, implementation considerations, and business impacts.

## Robotics and Automation

### Kitchen Robotics

**Current State (2025-2026)**

**Fry Station Automation**
- Miso Robotics Flippy Fry Station
- Automates french fries, onion rings, chicken, tacos
- Processes 100+ baskets per hour (almost twice human speed)
- Half the size, twice as fast as previous version
- Installs overnight in just a few hours (75% reduction)
- Backed by Ecolab, NVIDIA collaboration
- Piloting at White Castle, Jack in the Box

**Burger Assembly**
- ABB Robotics + BurgerBots
- IRB 360 FlexPicker and YuMi collaborative robot
- Precise and quick meal assembly
- Integrated inventory monitoring
- 27 seconds per burger
- Real-time inventory tracking
- High-speed topping selection with QR code guidance

**Autonomous Restaurants**
- Circus SE + Mangal Franchise
- World's first fully autonomous restaurant chain
- 500 CA-1 robots rolling out Fall 2025
- Potential scale to 2,400 locations across Europe, Turkey, Japan
- AI-robotics and AI-software powered
- Turkish cuisine focus
- High-volume, high-quality, efficient operations

**Pot Cooking Automation**
- Kitchen Robotics "Beastro"
- First robotic kitchen in university setting (FIU)
- Feeds 600 students daily
- Capable of cooking everything traditionally using pots
- Pasta bolognese, stir fries, chicken salad, omelets
- Four induction plates
- Two rows of feeder tubes (12 solid foods, 11 liquids)
- Robot arm removes cooking pot and slides to staff

**Fast Food Automation**
- Kernel Foods + KUKA
- KR 10 AGILUS R1100 robot
- NSF H1 food-grade lubricant
- 10 kg payload, 1100 mm reach
- Expediter role - traffic director
- Loads trays, moves to oven, removes, stacks
- Reliable and repeatable

## Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR)

### AR Dining Experiences

**Current Implementations**

**Cacao Roca RX (El Celler de Can Roca)**

- "Magical Journey Through Cocoa"
- Multisensory journey during dessert
- Combines haute cuisine with advanced technology
- Tablet-controlled by restaurant team
- Hygienic and comfortable glasses
- Simultaneous experience for all diners

**Shanghai AR Art Restaurant**
- World's first full-sensory AR art restaurant
- XREAL Air² Ultra AR glasses
- "3A × 5S" experience (AI, AR, Art × 5 Senses)
- Dalí-inspired elephants, De Chirico dancers, Rousseau florals
- Spatial computing, not projection mapping
- Real-time movement tracking
- 12 guests per session (groups of six)
- RMB680 lunch, RMB980 dinner
- 1,050 square meter space
- Digital gallery hybrid

**Le Petit Chef**
- 3D table projection experience
- Tiny animated chef cooks on plate
- 133 locations in 51 countries
- Story-driven experience per course

**Taste of China (Shanghai)**
- 270° panoramic view

## Blockchain Technology

### Supply Chain Applications

**Current Implementations**

**Toks Restaurant Chain (Mexico)**
- SIMBA Chain blockchain solution
- Track coffee beans from farm to consumer
- QR code for each bag of coffee
- Physical attributes tracked (humidity, weight)
- Immutable ledger for quality validation
- Increased farmer income by 700%
- Reduced intermediaries
- 200+ restaurants across Mexico

**University of Missouri Research**
- Blockchain for food traceability
- RFID labels scanned at each supply chain stage
- QR code on menu for consumer access
- Unchangeable blockchain ensures information integrity
- Consumers willing to pay premium for transparency
- Food safety most influential decision factor
- Trust enhances dining experience and restaurant reputation

**YumChain**
- Operating system for world's kitchens
- Recipe sourcing and deployment
- Automated on-chain royalties
- Replaces audit model with verification
- Every plate served is verifiable on-chain event
- Royalties flow automatically
- No auditors required
- Recipe version control
- Cross-border by default

## Key Insights

1. **Robotics Maturing**: Kitchen robotics moving from experimental to scalable industrial products
2. **Automation ROI**: Vendors claim day one ROI, but implementation requires significant investment
3. **AR/VR Premium**: AR/VR experiences command premium pricing and create differentiation
4. **Blockchain Transparency**: Blockchain supply chain traceability builds consumer trust and commands premium pricing
5. **Bitcoin Payments**: Lightning Network Bitcoin payments can reduce processing fees by 50%
6. **Human-AI Collaboration**: Future is human-AI collaboration, not full replacement
7. **Technology Convergence**: Multiple technologies will converge in future restaurant operations
8. **Adoption Timeline**: 5 years for most restaurants to have some form of robotic automation
9. **Niche to Mainstream**: AR/VR currently niche but moving toward mainstream adoption
10. **Regulatory Impact**: Regulatory frameworks will shape technology adoption pace

## Conclusion

Emerging technologies are transforming the restaurant industry at an accelerating pace. Robotics and automation address labor challenges and improve consistency, AR/VR create premium differentiated experiences, and blockchain provides transparency and efficiency in supply chains and payments. While implementation challenges exist, the competitive advantages and operational benefits are driving adoption. RESTAURANT_ERP should prepare for technology integration through modular architecture, API-first design, and flexible systems that can accommodate emerging technologies as they mature.
